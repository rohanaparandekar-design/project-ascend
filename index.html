Built by students. Driven by purpose.

Project Ascend started with a simple belief: the people who want to give and the families who need it most shouldn't be separated by logistics. We're a group of students from Mercer Island High School who decided to close that gap ourselves. We pick up donations directly from your door, sort everything by hand, and deliver to refugee families through our partners at Kandelia, ReWA, and World Relief Western Washington.

Our Mission

Every pickup we complete, every bag we sort, and every delivery we make is a step toward a community where new arrivals feel welcomed, supported, and at home. We believe that showing up is the most powerful thing a neighbor can do.

How It Works

Free pickup. Direct delivery.

We remove every barrier between your generosity and a family in need.

STEP 01

Sign Up

Fill out our quick form and tell us what you have to donate — any gently used items are welcome.

STEP 02

We Schedule Pickup

Our team reaches out to arrange a free doorstep pickup at a time that works for you.

STEP 03

We Sort and Prepare

Volunteers sort donations by category and condition, labeling items for each partner organization.

STEP 04

Direct Delivery

We deliver directly to Kandelia, ReWA, or World Relief — reaching families who need it most.

Our Partners

We work with three established nonprofits deeply rooted in the refugee community across greater Seattle. Every item we collect goes directly through their networks to families rebuilding their lives.
